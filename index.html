About Me

Hello! I am Justin Jago, bassist and student coder. I am chiefly known for my work playing bass in London's West End for musical theatre spectaculars.
The longest running show I perfomred in was 'Taboo - the Boy George Musical' which ran for 3 years in cluding a National tour for a year. Celebreties working with the show included:

  • Boy George
  • Julien Clarey
  • Matt Lucus
  • Mark Little

The show was set in London in the 1980's and portraied many colourful characters of the New Romanitc, arts and music scenes including:

  • Boy George
  • Leigh Bowery
  • Phillip Salon
  • Marolyn
  • Steve Strange
